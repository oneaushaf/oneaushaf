About Strivee

Strivee is a web app, developed to help client find nearby services, and service provider to get more clients. The user, wether the client or service provider will both have to make a registration to the app in order to use app (Peer to peer)

The app will only connect both party with the listed contact (for now it only support whatsapp). It also provide star rating system and client reviews to help clients determine their decision

Features and Capabilies

Listing the services provided

The first thing you'll see when visiting our web is list of services available, with an extra information of wether they are "online" services or not. Registered user will also get an extra list of services available in the registered area


Services and user account

Opening one of the service, you will find the detailed description the service, along with their score and reviews coming from past clients. Proceeding to order the service would open the contact of the service provider, and would enable the client to make review after the order has been completed.

In the profile, you can always customize your own data and services.
